Project Name: Actza
Description: This product is a wall-mounted Wi-Fi router that can be easily attached and detached from wall-mounted structure using a magnetic design. In addition to choosing from various materials for the case according to their preference, users can also customize the information displayed on the device's screen by selecting their desired screen face. I uploaded this project on Behance. So if you are interested in this project, please visit the site for more detailed information.
