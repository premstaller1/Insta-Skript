Project Name: A'Chive
Description: A'Chive; a product where you can change your shaving style as you wish while shaving. 
All you have to do is push the button. That's it!
Spare razor storage, stand and with specially designed packaging.

How to use:
It opens when you slide the button on your product upwards. When you push it to its fullest point, the head rotates and changes the shaving mode.
