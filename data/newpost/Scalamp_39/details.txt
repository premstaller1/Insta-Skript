Project Name: Scalamp
Description: The "Scalamp" is a homage to the timeless elegance of tubular steel design. The idea of making the frame continuous is meant to emphasize the endlessness or continuity of the timeless. The name "Scalamp" comesfrom the versatility of the product, which can be used both as a desk lamp and as a floor lamp.
