Project Name: Canopy
Description: Air Heater Re-imagined. Canopy is a simple, 3 speed, indoor air heater that blows air in all directions with a 360 radial vent in top. The form takes inspiration from the top wide canopy of Acacia trees.

Canopy tries to break the monotony of indoor heating appliance and brings and a fresh and friendly approach with a form that blends with modern contemporary homes.

Canopy features a tactile 3 speed marking on top making it easy to operate for everyone and a wooden knob that creates a good contrast and warmth with the saturated blue of the body.
