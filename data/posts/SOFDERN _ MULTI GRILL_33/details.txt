Project Name: SOFDERN | MULTI GRILL
Description: The SOFDERN Multi Grill is a multi-cooking appliance that can prepare a variety of dishes.
Its design, featuring soft colors and curved shape, enhances the culinary experience. The slide bar controller located at the bottom allows for easy operation with simple controls, and the intuitive icon interface has the convenience of easily recognizing hot to use operate it.

SOFDERN representing a series of kitchen appliances that approach the lives of modern individuals with a natural and cozy touch. it is designed to create harmony in our kitchen spaces.
