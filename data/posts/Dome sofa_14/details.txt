Project Name: Dome sofa
Description: Dome sofa is a modern reinterpretation of the iconic 1970s modular sofas. This collection
combines round, upholstered volumes with a solid wood base, creating a blend of coziness and
geometric elegance. Designed for the Portuguese brand @wewoodportuguesejoinery, 2024.
